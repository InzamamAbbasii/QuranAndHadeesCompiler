THE CHAPTER OF THE QURAIS

(CVI. Mecca.)

IN the name of the merciful and compassionate God. For the uniting of the Qurais; uniting them for the caravan of winter and summer.

So let them serve the Lord of this house who feeds them against hunger and makes them safe against fear.
