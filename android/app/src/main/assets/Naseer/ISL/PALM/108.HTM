THE CHAPTER OF EL KAUTHAR

(CVIII. Mecca.)

IN the name of the merciful and compassionate God.

Verily, we have given thee El Kauthar;

So pray to thy Lord and slaughter (victims).

Verily, he who hates thee shall be childless.
